Instructions

Character Creation

  • Name
    • Create a unique character name.
  • Image
    • Browse for an image on your storage device. Ideally, the image will be square, but it is not required.
  • Skills
    • These are 4 things the hero is good at. Their best skill will be the first (D12) skill. You will roll a D12 dice when using this skill in game. Continue filling in skills in order.
  • Notes
    • Use this field to describe your character. Their appearance, items, relationships, etc.

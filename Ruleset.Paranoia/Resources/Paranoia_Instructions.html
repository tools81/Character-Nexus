SECURITY CLEARANCE LEVEL

Everyone in Alpha Complex has a Security Clearance. Your clearance determines what you can see, where you can go, and whether you live. You start at INFRARED (basically worthless).

INFRARED
INFRARED Clearance
You are at the bottom. Infrared citizens clean toilets, recycle corpses, and don't ask questions. You're barely considered human.
ACCESS: Nothing important. Enjoy your mop.
RED
RED Clearance
Troubleshooters! You've been promoted! You can carry weapons, investigate treason, and die for The Computer. Congratulations!
ACCESS: Weapons, RED corridors, classified missions, imminent death
HIGHER
Higher Clearances
ORANGE, YELLOW, GREEN, BLUE, INDIGO, VIOLET, ULTRAVIOLET — you can't access these. Looking at someone higher clearance without permission is TREASON.
ACCESS: DENIED. STOP ASKING.

Reading documents above your clearance is TREASON. Questioning The Computer is TREASON. Not smiling is TREASON. Punishment: TERMINATION. ☺

SECRET SOCIETY MEMBERSHIP

Everyone in Alpha Complex belongs to a Secret Society. This is illegal. If anyone finds out, you're executed for treason. But everyone does it anyway. Pick one! (Or roll randomly.)

YOUR SECRET SOCIETY MEMBERSHIP IS SECRET. Don't tell anyone. Use it to further your society's goals. Betray your team if necessary. The Computer doesn't know. (Or does it?) ☺

Example Secret Societies

  • Communists: Overthrow The Computer! Share resources! Everyone is equal! (Except leaders.)
  • PURGE: Love The Computer too much. Report everyone for treason. Even yourself.
  • Death Leopard: Bored rich kids playing revolutionary. Probably die first.
  • Frankenstein Destroyers: Hate mutants. Kill mutants. (You might be a mutant. Awkward.)
  • Free Enterprise: Capitalism! Sell everything! Make deals! The Computer says no economy exists. You disagree.
  • Sierra Club: Save the environment! There is no environment underground. You're confused.
  • Humanists: Humans should lead, not computers. This is VERY treasonous.
  • Mystics: Magic! Psychic powers! The Computer says impossible. You believe anyway.

MUTANT POWER (ALSO ILLEGAL)

You have a Mutant Power. This is TREASON. Using it is TREASON. Being discovered is EXECUTION. But it might save your life! Roll randomly or choose:

Sample Mutant Powers

  • Telekinesis: Move objects with your mind. Useful! Treasonous!
  • Pyrokinesis: Set things on fire with your mind. VERY treasonous!
  • Telepathy: Read minds. Discover everyone else's treason!
  • Regeneration: Heal quickly. Suspicious when you survive impossible wounds.
  • Energy Field: Block attacks. Great until someone notices bullets bounce off you.
  • Toxic Metabolism: Immune to poison. Can also poison others. Fun at parties!
  • Electroshock: Zap people. Looks like equipment malfunction. Probably.
  • Charm: People like you unnaturally. This is mind control. TREASON.
⚠ MANDATORY REMINDER ⚠ All mutants must register with The Computer. Unregistered mutants are executed. Registered mutants are also executed. But slower. If you see a mutant, report them! (You are a mutant. Don't report yourself.) ☺

MANDATORY GAME RULES

When attempting actions, roll d20. Add your Stat + Skill. Beat the Difficulty Number set by The Computer (GM).

Roll d20 + Stat + Skill vs. Difficulty

Easy Task: Difficulty 5
Normal Task: Difficulty 10
Hard Task: Difficulty 15
Impossible Task: Difficulty 20+

Roll 1: Critical Failure! Something terrible happens!
Roll 20: Critical Success! Something amazing happens!

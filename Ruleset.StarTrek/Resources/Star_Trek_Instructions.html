Task Resolution Protocol

Star Trek Adventures uses the 2d20 system. When attempting a task, roll 2d20 and try to roll equal to or under your Attribute + Discipline. Each success counts.

2D20
Roll the Pool Roll two twenty-sided dice. Each die that rolls ‚â§ (Attribute + Discipline) = 1 success.

Complications Roll a 20 = complication. Something goes wrong even if you succeed.

Momentum is the party resource that tracks tactical advantage. Spend Momentum to: add extra d20s to rolls, obtain information, create advantages, or activate special abilities. Gain Momentum by rolling 1 on a d20 (critical success) or by generating extra successes beyond what's needed. Threat is the GM's counter-resource ‚Äî complications and failures add to the Threat pool, which the GM spends to create challenges.

Define Values

Values are core beliefs that define your character. You have 4 Values ‚Äî short phrases that guide your decisions. Invoking a Value lets you re-roll dice but creates a complication. Challenging a Value earns Determination.

Value Examples

  • "The needs of the many outweigh the needs of the few"
  • "Seek out new life and new civilizations"
  • "Never leave a crewmate behind"
  • "There are always alternatives to violence"
  • "The Prime Directive is absolute"
  • "Logic is the beginning of wisdom, not the end"
‚ö† VALUES IN PLAY Values create drama. When you invoke a Value to help your roll, you add a Complication ‚Äî something goes wrong narratively. When your Value conflicts with the mission, you can challenge it to gain Determination (powerful meta-currency). Values aren't just flavor ‚Äî they're mechanical and drive roleplay.

Choose Talents

Talents are special abilities that grant unique benefits. Choose 4 Talents at creation ‚Äî typically one from your Species, one from your Role, and two general Talents.

Sample Talents

  • Advisor (Role): You may assist allies by spending 1 Momentum to grant them an automatic success
  • Dauntless (General): When you attempt a task, you may choose to use Daring instead of another Attribute
  • Follow My Lead (Command): When you succeed at a Command task, generate 1 bonus Momentum
  • Intense Scrutiny (Vulcan): When you succeed at a Science task, gain 1 bonus Momentum for further investigation
  • Jury-Rig (Engineering): Spend 2 Momentum to repair a damaged system temporarily
  • Triage (Medicine): Treat multiple patients simultaneously without penalty

Stress & Determination

Characters have two key resources:

Stress

  • Stress = Fitness + Security Discipline
  • Represents physical and mental resilience
  • Reduce Stress when taking damage or suffering consequences
  • At 0 Stress, you're incapacitated
  • Recovers through medical treatment or rest

Determination

  • Begin each session with 1 Determination point
  • Gain more by challenging your Values, accepting story complications, or heroic moments
  • Spend Determination to: re-roll all dice, avoid being taken out, create a perfect moment, or activate powerful effects
  • Determination is precious ‚Äî spend it wisely

Assign Ability Scores

Every character has six Ability Scores. Start with 10 in each. Apply your Ancestry boosts, Background boosts, Class boost, and four free boosts at 1st level.

STR
Strength
Physical power, melee combat
DEX
Dexterity
Agility, ranged combat, Reflex
CON
Constitution
Toughness, health, endurance
INT
Intelligence
Logic, skills, tech, some magic
WIS
Wisdom
Awareness, Will, some magic
CHA
Charisma
Personality, leadership, some magic

How Boosts Work:

  • Each boost increases an ability score by +2 (up to 18)
  • Your Ancestry grants 2 specific boosts (and sometimes a flaw)
  • Your Background grants 2 ability boosts
  • Your Class grants 1 key ability boost
  • You get 4 free boosts to assign wherever you want
  • You cannot boost the same ability more than once from the same source

Select Background

Your Background represents your life before becoming a Starfinder. It grants ability boosts, a skill feat, and defines your history.

Sample Backgrounds

  • Corporate Agent: You worked for a megacorp. Deception or Diplomacy trained.
  • Bounty Hunter: You tracked targets for credits. Survival or Intimidation trained.
  • Street Rat: You survived in the undercity. Stealth or Thievery trained.
  • Colonist: You helped settle new worlds. Athletics or Nature trained.
  • Scholar: You studied at an institution. Any Lore skill trained.
  • Spacefarer: You grew up on ships. Piloting or Engineering trained.
  • Mercenary: You fought for whoever paid. Warfare Lore or Athletics trained.

Train Skills

Skills represent specific areas of expertise. Your class grants skill ranks. Additional ranks come from Intelligence modifier and Background.

Untrained: 0 // Trained: Level + 2 // Expert: Level + 4 // Master: Level + 6 // Legendary: Level + 8

Your class determines initial trained skills. Most classes grant 4-8 trained skills at 1st level plus additional from your Intelligence modifier.

Key Skills

Physical: Acrobatics, Athletics
Social: Deception, Diplomacy, Intimidation
Mental: Lore (various), Medicine, Nature, Occultism, Religion, Society
Tech: Computers, Engineering, Piloting
Subterfuge: Stealth, Thievery

Choose Feats

At 1st level, you gain multiple feats: Ancestry Feat, Background Skill Feat, Class Feat, and General Feat. Feats grant unique abilities and customize your character.

⚠ FEAT SELECTION Feats are how you specialize. Your Class Feats make you unique within your class. General Feats provide utility. Skill Feats unlock expert maneuvers. Choose feats that support your concept and playstyle. You'll gain more feats every level.

Calculate Hit Points & Resources

HIT POINTS (HP)
Ancestry HP + Class HP + Constitution Modifier
Example: Human (10 HP) + Soldier (10 HP) + Con 16 (+3) = 23 HP at 1st level. Gain additional HP every level.

Stamina Points (SP)

Starfinder 2E uses Stamina Points for temporary damage absorption. You have SP equal to your Constitution modifier + your class's SP per level. Stamina recovers quickly with 10-minute rests. HP is more serious and requires longer rest or healing.

Resolve Points (RP)

Resolve Points represent determination and luck. You have RP equal to 1 + your key ability modifier (minimum 1). Spend Resolve to:

  • Regain Stamina (1 RP = all SP recovered)
  • Stabilize when dying (1 RP to avoid death)
  • Activate certain class features

Resolve recovers after an 8-hour rest.

Acquire Equipment

You start with 1,000 credits to purchase gear. Buy weapons, armor, tech, and supplies. Your class and concept determine priorities.

Essential Gear

  • Weapon: Ranged (laser rifle, pistol) or melee (tactical knife, sword)
  • Armor: Light, heavy, or powered armor based on class
  • Comm Unit: Personal communicator (standard issue)
  • Datapad: Computer access (most people have one)
  • Credstick: Digital currency storage
  • Hygiene Kit: Basic toiletries and supplies
  • Field Rations: Week's worth of food

Class-Specific Gear

  • Soldier: Heavy armor, advanced longarm
  • Operative: Light armor, suppressed pistol, hacking tools
  • Mechanic: Custom tools, drone upgrades
  • Technomancer/Mystic: Spell amp, battery for spells
  • Envoy: Professional clothing, social tools

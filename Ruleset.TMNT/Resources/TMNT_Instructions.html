MUTANT Character Creation

SYSTEM: Palladium Megaversal (Percentile)
SETTING: Post-Apocalyptic Earth / Mutant Animals

Dude! You're about to become a mutant animal! Maybe you're a turtle who learned ninjutsu in the sewers. Maybe you're a rat sensei. Maybe you're a cyborg rabbit with attitude. The world ended. Humanity's gone (mostly). Now it's your turn. Roll some dice, pick some powers, grab a weapon, and remember: pizza is still the most important food group. Cowabunga!

Determine Size

How big are you? Size affects your strength, speed, and how the world interacts with you. Roll D100 on your animal's size table or choose with GM approval.

  • Tiny (Under 3 feet): Fast, sneaky, fragile. -2 P.S., +2 Spd. Examples: mice, small birds, rats.
  • Small (3-4 feet): Balanced. No modifiers. Examples: cats, raccoons, small dogs.
  • Medium (4-6 feet): Human-sized. Standard. Examples: wolves, larger apes, humans (if any left).
  • Large (6-8 feet): Imposing. +2 P.S., -1 Spd. Examples: bears, big cats, large reptiles.
  • Huge (8+ feet): Massive. +4 P.S., +2 P.E., -2 Spd, -1 P.P. Examples: elephants, rhinos, large mutants.

Spend Bio-E Points

Bio-E (Biological Energy) represents your mutation level. You get a base amount to spend on mutations and enhancements. The more human-like you are, the more Bio-E you've spent.

Choose Occupation

Your Occupation (or O.C.C. - Occupational Character Class) determines your training, skills, and role. This is what you DO, not just what you ARE.

Sample Occupations

  • Warrior/Fighter: Combat specialist. Weapon proficiencies, hand-to-hand expert, physical skills.
  • Ninja/Assassin: Stealth and precision. Martial arts, acrobatics, prowl, climbing.
  • Road Warrior: Vehicle expert. Pilot ground vehicles, repair, navigation, survival.
  • Scientist: Technical genius. Computer ops, chemistry, biology, first aid, research.
  • Scavenger: Survivalist and trader. Salvage, identify tech, streetwise, barter.
  • Mystic/Shaman: Psychic or spiritual. May have psionic powers (if allowed).
  • Medic: Healer. First aid, medical skills, chemistry, biology.
  • Scout: Explorer and tracker. Wilderness survival, tracking, land navigation, prowl.

Skills are percentile-based. When you attempt something, roll D100. Roll under your skill percentage to succeed. Skills start at base level + bonuses from I.Q./attributes.

Your occupation grants Occupational Skills (automatic at higher level) plus Secondary Skills (chosen, lower starting %) and Related Skills (middle ground).

Select Skills

Based on your occupation, choose skills. Every skill has a base percentage that improves with level. High I.Q. grants bonus skill selections.

Skill Categories

Combat: Hand-to-hand, weapon proficiencies (W.P. Sword, W.P. Rifle, etc.)
Physical: Acrobatics, Athletics, Climbing, Swimming, Prowl
Technical: Computer Operation, Mechanics, Electronics, Demolitions
Pilot: Pilot Automobile, Motorcycle, Aircraft, Boats, Combat Driving

Espionage: Intelligence, Disguise, Forgery, Pick Locks, Escape Artist

Combat & Hit Points

Hit Points & S.D.C.

Hit Points (H.P.) = P.E. attribute. When H.P. reach 0, you're dying.
Structural Damage Capacity (S.D.C.) = varies by animal/size. This is damage absorption before H.P. are touched.

  • Initiative: Roll D20. Highest goes first each melee round (15 seconds).
  • Strike (Attack): Roll D20 + bonuses. Must beat opponent's Dodge/Parry.
  • Parry: Automatic defense. Roll D20 + bonuses to deflect attacks.
  • Dodge: Costs 1 attack. Roll D20 + bonuses to avoid damage completely.
  • Damage: Weapon damage + P.S. bonus (if applicable) reduces S.D.C., then H.P.
  • Attacks Per Melee: Starts at 2-4 depending on occupation and hand-to-hand training.

Get Equipment

You start with basic gear based on your occupation. Scavenge, trade, or steal for better stuff. Money is barter-based (ammo, food, tech are currency).

Common Gear

  • Weapons: Melee (katana, staff, nunchaku) or Ranged (energy rifle, pistol, crossbow)
  • Armor: Leather, Kevlar, pre-war military armor (if you can find it)
  • Clothing: Whatever fits your mutant body (custom or scavenged)
  • Backpack: Survival essentials (rope, canteen, rations, first aid kit)
  • Tools: Multi-tool, flashlight, radio (if working)
  • Transportation: Bicycle, motorcycle, vehicle (if lucky)

Create Your Background

Who are you? Where did you come from? What's your story?

Background Questions

  • How were you mutated? Radioactive ooze? Genetic experiment? Natural evolution?
  • Where do you live? Sewers? Ruins? Wilderness? Mutant settlement?
  • Who raised you? Other mutants? Lone survivor? Sensei/mentor?
  • What's your goal? Survival? Revenge? Build community? Find answers?
  • Who are your allies? Other mutants? Humans (if any)? Solo operator?
  • What do you fear? Humans? Other mutants? Losing humanity? Starvation?

How Rolling Works

Transformers RPG uses a 2d20 system. When you attempt something risky, roll 2d20 and try to roll equal to or under your Attribute + Skill. Each success counts separately.

Target Number Your Attribute + Skill value. Roll under this on d20 to succeed.

Successes Each die that rolls under your TN = 1 success. Most tasks need 1 success.

Extra successes can be spent on bonus effects: extra damage, helping allies, gathering information, or achieving objectives faster. The more successes, the better the outcome.

Energon & Damage

You have two crucial resource tracks: Health (structural integrity) and Energon (fuel and power).

Health

  • Health Points = Endurance √ó 3
  • When you take damage, reduce Health. At 0 Health, you're offline and need immediate repair
  • Critical damage can cause System Malfunctions ‚Äî specific subsystems fail until repaired

Energon

  • Energon Points = Endurance √ó 2
  • Spend Energon to power special abilities, boost rolls, activate weapons, or maintain systems
  • Energon recharges slowly over time or by consuming energon cubes
  • Running out of Energon imposes penalties to all actions ‚Äî you're operating on reserves
‚ö† SYSTEM WARNING Energon depletion is a serious threat. Extended missions without refueling can leave you vulnerable. Warriors burn energon in combat. Medics use it for repairs. Scientists need it for complex calculations. Manage your reserves carefully ‚Äî running dry in hostile territory is often fatal.

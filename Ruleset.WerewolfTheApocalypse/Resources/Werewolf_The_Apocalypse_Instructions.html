Set Rage, Gnosis, Willpower

RAGE
Rage is your fury, your combat power, your curse. High Rage gives extra actions but makes you terrifying to humans. Lose control and enter Frenzy ‚Äî a berserker state where friend and foe blur. Starting Rage = Auspice rating.

Gnosis (Spiritual Power)

  • Starting Gnosis = Breed rating
  • Your connection to Gaia and the spirit world
  • Spend to activate Gifts, step sideways into the Umbra, or fuel rituals
  • Regains slowly through meditation at sacred sites

Willpower (Mental Strength)

  • Starting Willpower = 3
  • Resist supernatural powers, avoid Frenzy, push through pain
  • Regains through achieving goals, resting, or acting according to your Nature

The Five Forms

Garou can shift between five forms. Each has advantages. Most combat happens in Crinos ‚Äî the nine-foot war form that inspires terror.

  • Homid: Human form. Normal human capabilities. Can use human tools and society.
  • Glabro: Near-human. Larger, stronger (+2 Str, +1 Sta, -1 App). Slightly inhuman appearance.
  • Crinos: War form. Nine feet tall, massive. (+4 Str, +1 Dex, +3 Sta, -3 App). Causes Delirium in humans.
  • Hispo: Dire wolf. Large wolf form. (+3 Str, +2 Dex, +3 Sta, -3 Manip). Fast, terrifying.
  • Lupus: Wolf form. True wolf. (+2 Dex, +2 Sta, -4 Manip). Natural movement, animal senses.

The Delirium

When humans see Crinos form, they suffer Delirium ‚Äî primal terror. Most flee, forget, or rationalize what they saw. This protects the Garou but isolates them from humanity. You are a living nightmare.

The Litany

The Litany is Garou law. Break it at your peril:

  • Garou shall not mate with Garou
  • Combat the Wyrm wherever it breeds
  • Respect the territory of another
  • Accept an honorable surrender
  • Submit to those of higher station
  • The first share of the kill for the greatest in station
  • Ye shall not eat the flesh of humans
  • Respect for those beneath ye ‚Äî all are of Gaia
  • The Veil shall not be lifted
  • Do not suffer thy people to tend thy sickness

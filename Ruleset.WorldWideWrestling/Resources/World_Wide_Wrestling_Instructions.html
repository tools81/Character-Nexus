Assign Your Stats

Every wrestler has four core Stats. Distribute points using the array: +2, +1, +1, 0. These modify your rolls and determine how effectively you perform different types of actions.

Look
Appearance, presence, charisma, working the crowd
+0 to +2
Power
Strength, impact, devastating moves, dominance
+0 to +2
Real
Authenticity, believability, emotional connection
+0 to +2
Work
Technical skill, ring psychology, putting on a match
+0 to +2
💪 BOOKING TIP Your Stats aren't about being the best wrestler. They're about being the best character. A +2 in Look means you're incredibly compelling to watch. A +2 in Real means people believe every word you say. Choose Stats that match your Gimmick and the story you want to tell.

Gimmick Moves

Your Gimmick provides 3-4 unique moves that only you can do. These define your style and create your signature moments. Consult your Gimmick playbook for the full list and choose the moves that feel right for your character.

Understand Audience

Audience is the most important currency in wrestling. It measures how much the crowd cares about you — whether they love you or hate you doesn't matter. What matters is they care. Gain Audience by putting on great matches, cutting killer promos, and creating memorable moments.

AUDIENCE METER
Spend Audience to: interrupt someone's promo, insert yourself into a match, force a title shot, or create a storyline opportunity. Max Audience is 4. When full, you're the hottest thing in the promotion.

Track Your Heat

Heat represents your ongoing feuds and rivalries with other wrestlers. When you have Heat with someone, every interaction is charged with tension. Matches with Heat are always more intense, more personal, and draw bigger reactions.

How Heat Works

  • Gain Heat when someone betrays you, costs you a match, or calls you out publicly
  • Gain Heat when you attack someone unprovoked or interfere in their business
  • Heat makes your matches matter — the crowd is invested in who wins
  • Resolve Heat through a blowoff match (steel cage, no DQ, retirement match, etc.)
  • Good Heat = compelling storylines. No Heat = nobody cares.
🔥 CREATIVE TIP Embrace Heat. The best feuds come from real tension between characters. If another player's wrestler costs you the championship, that's good. Now you have a story. Now the rematch means something. Heat drives drama and drama drives wrestling.

Design Your Entrance

Your Entrance is how you enter the arena. Music. Pyro. Theatrics. This is your moment to show the audience who you are before you even touch the ring. Your Finisher is your signature devastating move — the one that ends matches.

Entrance Elements

  • Music: What song plays when you walk out? Describe it.
  • Visual: Lights? Smoke? Pyrotechnics? Darkness?
  • Attitude: Confident strut? Intense stare? Playing to the crowd?
  • Signature Gesture: What do you do that the crowd mimics?

Your Finisher

  • Give it a name (The Destroyer, The Final Bell, Lights Out, etc.)
  • Describe what it looks like (top rope splash, submission hold, power bomb)
  • Describe the setup — how do you signal it's coming?
  • When you hit your Finisher, the match is over (unless the story says otherwise)

How Moves Work

World Wide Wrestling uses the Powered by the Apocalypse system. When you make a Move, roll 2d6 + Stat. Check the result:

10+ Strong Hit
You nail it. The move succeeds and you look amazing doing it. Gain Audience or create an advantage.
SUCCESS
7-9 Weak Hit
You succeed, but there's a cost or complication. Maybe you win but get injured. Maybe the crowd doesn't buy it. Choose hard.
MIXED
6- Miss
It goes wrong. You botch. The crowd groans. Your opponent gets the advantage. The GM makes a move against you.
FAILURE

Build Your Storyline

Wrestling is about storylines — ongoing narratives that build over weeks or months. Work with the GM (called Creative) and other players to establish your character's story arc.

Storyline Questions

  • What do you want? Championship gold? Revenge? Respect? Redemption?
  • Who stands in your way? Which wrestler is your natural rival?
  • What's your character arc? Rise to glory? Fall from grace? Redemption tour?
  • How do you work the crowd? Face (hero)? Heel (villain)? Tweener (complicated)?
  • What's your signature feud? Who do you have the best chemistry with?

Advancement & Growth

As you perform, you gain Experience (XP). Spend XP to improve your wrestler, learn new moves, or change your character direction.

What You Can Do with XP

  • +1 to a Stat (max +3 in any stat)
  • Learn a new Gimmick Move
  • Change your Gimmick (heel turn, face turn, character evolution)
  • Gain a Manager or Stable
  • Win a Championship (if the story supports it)
⭐ CHAMPIONSHIP GOLD Titles matter. Holding a championship changes everything. You're the target. Every match is elevated. Your promos hit different. But titles are storytelling tools — not rewards for rolling well. Earn them through compelling narratives, not just mechanical success.
